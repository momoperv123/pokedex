Generation 1 Starter Pokemon
Favorite Pokemon

Lugia is a legendary Psychic/Flying-type Pokémon from the second generation of the Pokémon games, known for its massive size, silver coloration, and the ability to control the elements of the sea and the sky. It is often depicted as a guardian of the seas and a symbol of balance and harmony in the Pokémon world.

Reshiram is a Dragon/Fire-type legendary Pokémon from the fifth generation of the Pokémon games, known for its white and blue coloration and fiery features. It is associated with the values of truth and ideals in the Pokémon lore, and has the ability to generate flames from its tail and wings.

Lunala is a legendary Psychic/Ghost-type Pokémon from the seventh generation of the Pokémon games, known for its bat-like appearance and crescent moon-shaped wings. It is associated with the moon and the world of dreams, and has the ability to create portals that allow it to travel through dimensions.

Rayquaza is a legendary Dragon/Flying-type Pokémon from the third generation of the Pokémon games, known for its serpentine appearance, green and yellow coloration, and the ability to soar through the ozone layer. It is considered a powerful and revered deity in the Pokémon world, often associated with the balance of nature and the sky.
